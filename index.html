Behr Natural Stain will be applied to your wood side or RV gate. Staining your gate seals out the elements and the sun's harmful UV rays, for up to 6 years. We offer 3 varieties of finishes: Natural Transparent, Semi-Transparent, and Solid Redwood. Your gate can look the way you want it to, all while protecting your investment.

Both sides of your gate will be wiped down to remove dirt and debris, drop cloths are placed under your gate to catch any drippings, finally stain is applied to both sides of your gate with care.

The two step process to remove the weathered look requires the use of your water hose and my special cleaning solution. Care is taken during application, there will be no mess left behind. To continue, the gate needs to dry before stain can be applied. We will return later that day or the next morning to complete. It will be worth the wait!!
